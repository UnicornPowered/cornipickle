What's the problem?

Two squares overlap (let's pretend they shouldn't). Try moving the squares around and re-evaluate the property.

We say that $x extends outside $y when ( ( ($x's right is greater than $y's width) Or (0 is greater than $y's left) ) Or ( ($x's bottom is greater than $y's height) Or (0 is greater than $y's top) ) ). # We define the negation to increase readability We say that $x doesn't extend outside $y when ( Not ($x extends outside $y) ).
